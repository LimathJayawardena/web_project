Terms and Conditions

Welcome to NSBM Food Nest. By accessing this web application, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms and conditions of use.

1. Order Process

All orders placed through the NSBM Food Nest platform are subject to availability. Presenting a valid NSBM Student or Staff ID is mandatory upon collecting your order.

2. Payment & Pricing

All prices listed on the application are in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR) and are fixed. Payments must be finalized at the campus pickup point unless otherwise specified in your receipt.

3. Cancellations

Once an order has been successfully processed and a receipt generated, cancellations cannot be made via the web portal. Please contact the canteen administration directly for any adjustments.

4. Dietary Information

While we strive to provide accurate descriptions of our food items, NSBM Food Nest cannot guarantee that any item is entirely free of allergens. Please inquire directly if you have severe dietary restrictions.

5. System Usage

This portal is specifically developed for the NSBM Green University community. Unauthorized access to the Admin Portal or misuse of the cart system is strictly prohibited.
